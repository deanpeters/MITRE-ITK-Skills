## Pitfall Check (before you close)

- [ ] We fully generalized in Step 2 (no specifics left anchoring us to familiar solutions)
- [ ] We did not over-generalize into triviality
- [ ] We reintroduced specifics in Step 4 (not left with just a clever metaphor)
- [ ] We're treating the analogy as a hypothesis to prototype, not as the answer
- [ ] This problem actually warrants TRIZ (no established best practice or competitor benchmark would suffice)
- [ ] Multiple voices drove the generalization — not just the loudest
