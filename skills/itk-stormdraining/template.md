## Step 2: Drain the Collection

Apply the Five Rules of Stormdraining as you remove items. Reminder of the rules:

1. **Everything Is on the Table** — no sacred cows; every item is fair game.
2. **Delete Is the Default** — items must justify survival, not deletion.
3. **Build on Other People's Deletions** — one cut points to the next; practice "Yes, and…"
4. **Make It Fun** — celebrate and encourage the courage to delete.
5. **When You Delete Something, Really Delete It** — no shadow backlog, no "parked for later."
