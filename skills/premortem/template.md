# Premortem Template

Use this template when kicking off a high-stakes initiative, epic, or launch to imagine a future failure, surface hidden assumptions, and convert the riskiest failure drivers into owned, scheduled mitigations.

## Step 1 — Describe the Failure

> Imagine it is two years in the future. This project has *utterly and completely* failed. Spend a few minutes writing privately, then discuss as a group. Focus on **outcomes and attributes**, not behaviors or causes. Use vivid, extreme language. Don't self-edit.
