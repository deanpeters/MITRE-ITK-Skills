## Assess Stakeholder's Motivations

*Answer in any order.*

- **What does this stakeholder need?** [their needs]
- **What does this stakeholder want?** [their wants]
- **What are their goals or success measures?** [goals]
- **What are their concerns, fears, or pain points?** [concerns]
- **How much influence do they have over your effort's success?** [high / medium / low — explain]
- **How much does your effort impact them?** [high / medium / low — explain]
- **What is their current attitude (supportive, neutral, resistant)?** [attitude]

### Equity Lens

- **Are there power imbalances or access barriers affecting this stakeholder?** [notes]
- **Does this stakeholder represent a marginalized or historically excluded voice?** [yes / no — notes]
- **What action will you take to genuinely include them (not just check a box)?** [action]
