## Facilitator Notes & Reminders

- Invite junior or dissenting voices to share first to avoid senior anchoring.
- Give roses equal airtime—don't let thorns dominate.
- Keep framing blameless: focus on systems and processes, not people.
- Use **Trimming** to narrow down which buds the team commits to acting on.
