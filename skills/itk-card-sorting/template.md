## Step 1 — Prepare the Cards

Write each piece of information onto a card. Keep all cards at a consistent level of granularity (single word, phrase, or task). Assign each card an ID number for documentation.

| Card ID | Card Content | Granularity Level |
|---------|--------------|-------------------|
| 1 | [word, phrase, or sentence] | [word / phrase / task] |
| 2 | [card content] | [level] |
| 3 | [card content] | [level] |
| 4 | [card content] | [level] |
| 5 | [card content] | [level] |
| ... | [card content] | [level] |

> Keep total cards under ~40–50 to avoid participant fatigue.

## Step 3 — Run the Sort (Per Participant)

Ask the participant to sort cards one by one in a way that makes sense to them. Encourage them to think aloud. Capture notes below.
