# Mission & Vision Canvas Template

Use this template when a team is forming, pivoting, or needs clarity on purpose — it produces a co-authored Vision statement (future aspiration) and Mission statement (present activity), plus the link between them.

## Step 1: Orient the Group

- Session objective: [why we're running this canvas today]
- Reminder — **VISION** = future conditions in aspirational terms.
- Reminder — **MISSION** = present activities in concrete terms.
- We are starting with: [VISION / MISSION]

---

## Step 2: Quiet Individual Writing (4–6 minutes)

Each member writes responses privately before group discussion.

### Vision Canvas — Lettered Boxes

| Box | Prompt | Your Response |
|-----|--------|---------------|
| A | [What future do we want to create?] | [response] |
| B | [What conditions will exist when we succeed?] | [response] |
| C | [Who benefits and how?] | [response] |
| D | [Why does this future matter?] | [response] |

### Mission Canvas — Lettered Boxes

| Box | Prompt | Your Response |
|-----|--------|---------------|
| A | [What do we do today?] | [response] |
| B | [Who do we do it for?] | [response] |
| C | [How do we do it?] | [response] |
| D | [What value/result do we deliver?] | [response] |
| E | [Why do we exist?] | [response] |
| Bonus | [Don't miss this — the differentiating / underlying purpose] | [response] |

## Step 5: Quality Check & Test

| Quality Check | Vision | Mission |
|---------------|--------|---------|
| Is it specific (not a vague platitude)? | [yes/no] | [yes/no] |
| Vision = aspirational & future-oriented? | [yes/no] | n/a |
| Mission = concrete & present-oriented? | n/a | [yes/no] |
| Could it guide a real trade-off decision? | [yes/no] | [yes/no] |
| Does the whole team endorse it? | [yes/no] | [yes/no] |
| Distinguishable from a competitor's? | [yes/no] | [yes/no] |

**Reference example —**
Feeding America Vision: *A Hunger Free America* (aspirational, future-oriented)
Feeding America Mission: *To feed America's hungry through a nationwide network of member food banks and engage our country in the fight to end hunger* (concrete, present-oriented)
