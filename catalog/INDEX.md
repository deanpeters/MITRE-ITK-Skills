# MITRE ITK Skills — Catalog

27 tools across 5 innovation phases, sourced from the [MITRE Innovation Toolkit](https://itk.mitre.org/toolkit/tools-at-a-glance/).

**Total skills:** 27


## SCOPE (7 tools)

_Get clarity and consensus about the problem space, stakeholders, and broader context._

- [Community Map](../skills/itk-community-map/SKILL.md) — A fast way to capture and prioritize stakeholders. _beginner · 8+ people · 45+ minutes_
- [Culture Building Canvas](../skills/itk-culture-building-canvas/SKILL.md) — A framework based on “influence channels” that shape and define an organization’s culture. _intermediate · 4+ people · 60+ minutes_
- [Stakeholder Identify](../skills/itk-stakeholder-identify/SKILL.md) — Ideate a more comprehensive & representative set of relevant stakeholders to your project. _beginner · 2+ people · 60+ minutes_
- [Stakeholder Map](../skills/itk-stakeholder-map/SKILL.md) — Look across stakeholders and categorize them according to key variables (e.g., interest, influence, impact). _intermediate · 4+ people · 45+ minutes_
- [Stakeholder Power](../skills/itk-stakeholder-power/SKILL.md) — Use this canvas to quickly categorize and prioritize which stakeholders to engage based on power and impact. Highlight impacted stakeholders and ideate on how to elevate their roles on the effort _intermediate · 1+ people · 60+ minutes_
- [Stakeholder Quickstart](../skills/itk-stakeholder-quickstart/SKILL.md) — A quick way to begin developing a plan for effectively engaging a stakeholder. _intermediate · 1+ people · 60+ minutes_
- [System Map](../skills/itk-system-map/SKILL.md) — This tool captures the key roles, relationships, and dynamics of a system to enhance understanding of the big picture and potential disruptions to the system. _intermediate · 4+ people · 45+ minutes_

## DEFINE (3 tools)

_Clarify and prioritize the key problems and hypotheses you want to explore._

- [Mission Vision](../skills/itk-mission-vision/SKILL.md) — Develop clear statements about your organization’s future aspirations and present activities. _intermediate · 6+ people · 60+ minutes_
- [Premortem](../skills/itk-premortem/SKILL.md) — Frame and explore your problem by imagining a future scenario in which the proposed activity fails to achieve its objective. _intermediate · 6+ people · 60+ minutes_
- [Problem Framing](../skills/itk-problem-framing/SKILL.md) — Explore a problem space and formulate a robust problem statement to ensure you’re solving the right problem. _beginner · 2+ people · 45+ minutes_

## UNDERSTAND (7 tools)

_Explore the problem you've defined and gain user insights._

- [Card Sorting](../skills/itk-card-sorting/SKILL.md) — This hands-on activity allows participants to communicate and document their mental model and how they think about a set of information, creating a logical structure (e.g., relationships, sequences, timing). _advanced · 4+ people · 45+ minutes_
- [Journey Mapping](../skills/itk-journey-mapping/SKILL.md) — Visually synthesize and communicate a user’s end-to-end experience through actions, pain points, wins, and opportunities in a phased process. _advanced · 4+ people · 45+ minutes_
- [Painstorming](../skills/itk-painstorming/SKILL.md) — PAIN stands for Persona, Activities, Insights, and Needs – the four main research topics explored in this structured method for gathering insights about users or user identities. _beginner · 2+ people · 30+ minutes_
- [Personas](../skills/itk-personas/SKILL.md) — A descriptive model of a person (user, stakeholder, team member, etc), this tool is most often used to help a team define and understand the needs of its customer. _intermediate · 6+ people · 45+ minutes_
- [Service Blueprint](../skills/itk-service-blueprint/SKILL.md) — Outline and visualize a service to connect the user experience, or “frontstage,” to what happens behind-the-scenes, or “backstage.” _advanced · 5+ people · 60+ minutes_
- [Storyboarding](../skills/itk-storyboarding/SKILL.md) — Stories add a human element to design and data analysis activities. They foster empathy and let designers walk in the users’ shoes, increasing understanding of needs, activities, interests, and pain points. _intermediate · 4+ people · 60+ minutes_
- [Value Prop](../skills/itk-value-prop/SKILL.md) — Map user profiles (i.e., jobs, pains, and gains) to values (i.e., gain creators, products and services, pain relievers) to ensure that a product meets user needs. _intermediate · 4+ people · 45+ minutes_

## GENERATE (4 tools)

_Brainstorm and create solutions that help address the problems._

- [Bodystorming](../skills/itk-bodystorming/SKILL.md) — In this physical variant on brainstorming, participants use their body and inexpensive materials or objects to role-play and mimic interaction with a system, product, or experience. _intermediate · 6+ people · 45+ minutes_
- [Lotus Blossom](../skills/itk-lotus-blossom/SKILL.md) — Focus the power of brainstorming using a structured, visual representation of ideas—pushing you to fill out every box with new ideas. _beginner · 2+ people · 60+ minutes_
- [Mindmapping](../skills/itk-mindmapping/SKILL.md) — Generate insights into relationships between concepts in a flexible and engaging way to visualize information. _beginner · 2+ people · 30+ minutes_
- [Triz Prism](../skills/itk-triz-prism/SKILL.md) — Use known solutions to find new solutions to difficult problems. _beginner · 4+ people · 60+ minutes_

## EVALUATE (6 tools)

_Test or evaluate different solutions and iterate on them._

- [Prototyping](../skills/itk-prototyping/SKILL.md) — Developing an early version of a product to convey the look (form-appearance) and feel (function-behavior); can be static or dynamic in nature but is typically built quickly. _intermediate · 2+ people · 60+ minutes_
- [Retro Rundown](../skills/itk-retro-rundown/SKILL.md) — A structured, facilitated conversation that helps a team reflect on a completed activity or event by comparing what was expected with what happened and identifying concrete improvements. _beginner · 4+ people · 45+ minutes_
- [Rose Bud Thorn](../skills/itk-rose-bud-thorn/SKILL.md) — Visually categorize positive (rose), potential (bud), or negative (thorn) aspects of a topic (e.g., system, product, process). _beginner · 4+ people · 45+ minutes_
- [Simplicity Cycle](../skills/itk-simplicity-cycle/SKILL.md) — Equip users with a visual vocabulary for discussing complexity and simplicity in a design to assess and express the value of adding or removing design elements. _advanced · 6+ people · 60+ minutes_
- [Stormdraining](../skills/itk-stormdraining/SKILL.md) — The inverse of brainstorming, reduce a large collection of ideas, activities, or components to a smaller collection of the most valuable or promising ideas. _beginner · 2+ people · 60+ minutes_
- [Trimming](../skills/itk-trimming/SKILL.md) — Iteratively remove unnecessary elements from a design using a structured approach to reducing complexity to produce a more elegant, streamlined final product, process, or organization. _intermediate · 4+ people · 60+ minutes_
